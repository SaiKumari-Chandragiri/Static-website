FACILITIES

Our Lahari Old age homes offer a variety of facilities to ensure the well-being, comfort, and quality of life for elderly residents.

Accommodation

Private or shared rooms with basic amenities like beds, wardrobes, and bathrooms.Common areas such as lounges, dining rooms, and gardens.

Medical Care

Regular health check-ups,On-site nursing staff, Access to doctors and specialists,Emergency medical services,Medication management are providing.

Nutritions

In our old age home we are providing Balanced and nutritious meals, Special dietary plans for residents with specific health conditions,Assistance with feeding if needed.

Housekeeping & laundry

Regular cleaning and laundry services, Maintenance of the facility and individual rooms.

Counseling and Support Services

Psychological and emotional support, Counseling services for residents and their families.

Specialized Care

Dementia and Alzheimer's care units, Palliative and hospice care for terminally ill residents.

Recreational Activities

In our lahari old age home we are also conducting Social activities like games, movies, group outings, Exercise and fitness classes.

Safety and Security

our old age home will support 24/7 security and surveillance, Emergency call systems in rooms, Fire safety measures.

Transportation

our old age home will support Transport services for medical appointments, shopping trips and outings.
