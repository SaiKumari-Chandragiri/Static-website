Founders

The tradition of social service has a long and unbroken history. Through the ages, there have been mom and daughter who in their own quiet and unobtrusive manner dedicated themselves to the service of their fellowmen. They have worked tirelessly to remove injustice, relieve distress and mitigate suffering. Examples of their struggle to rescue men from brutal forces of cruelty, inhumanity and injustice are very inspiring and ennobling.

One such mom & daughter who sacrificed their life for the cause of humanity is the founders of LAHARI OLD AGE HOME, RADHIKA and LAHARI. They Established in 2011 with just 4 members, LAHARI OLD AGE HOME has now grown to be a highly reputed and revered social institution in India today.
