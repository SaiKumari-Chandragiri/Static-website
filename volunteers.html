Volunteers

Volunteers play a crucial role in supporting the work that we do at Lahari Old Age Home. We welcome volunteers who are passionate about making a positive difference in the lives of our elderly residents. As a volunteer, you can help us with a range of tasks, from spending time with residents and organizing activities to helping with administrative tasks and fundraising events. If you are interested in volunteering, please contact with this email it to somojigarilahari2003@gmail.com. We will review your email and get in touch with you to discuss the next steps. Please note that all volunteers must undergo a screening process. By volunteering your time and skills, you can make a meaningful impact in the lives of our residents and help us create a warm and welcoming environment that fosters dignity, respect, and care.

Thank you for considering volunteering at Lahari Old Age Home. We look forward to hearing from you.
